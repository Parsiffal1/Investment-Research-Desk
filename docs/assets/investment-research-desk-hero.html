The workflow is designed to feel like a local research desk rather than a one-shot prompt. Multiple analysts gather evidence, argument streams diverge, then the system compresses them into a final context object with explicit uncertainty.

Instead of pushing users toward action, the output stays at the level of research context: what is structurally supportive, what is conflicting, what needs monitoring, and what artifacts justify the view.
