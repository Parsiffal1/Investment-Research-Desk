# Investment Research Desk Hero V6 Script

## Goal
Refine the improved phase-based structure from v5, but make phase 3 feel more like an **elite research desk lineup** instead of a generic UI layout.

The final scene should communicate: this is a finance research product powered by a specialist analyst team.

## Core user memory after one viewing
A new GitHub visitor should retain these visual fragments:
- this is finance / investment research
- there is real market structure, not generic SaaS abstraction
- the product works through a multi-agent analyst team
- the team has four specialist roles:
  - Fundamental / Macro
  - News Impact
  - Sentiment
  - Technical
- this is research support, not execution

## Direction updates from v5
- Make phase 3 more like an institutional / elite research desk lineup
- Increase phase 3 scale so it occupies more of the frame
- Replace the central slogan with a more human invitation:
  - `Meet your analyst team`
- Add an icon to each agent card so phase 3 is not text-only
- Shorten the full animation slightly
- Make the hold times and motion a bit faster without becoming rushed or flickery

## Phase structure

### Phase 1 — Institutional brand reveal
- Keep the calm bank-grade opening.
- Keep the bottom line:
  - `Local-first financial research workflow · research context only`
- Let phase 1 establish polish and product category quickly.

### Phase 2 — Professional market structure field
- Keep the financial market surface.
- Show K-line / price structure, sparse tickers, macro pressure.
- Keep it cleaner and move through it slightly faster than v5.

### Phase 3 — Meet your analyst team
This is not a dashboard and not a workflow diagram.

Instead, make it feel like a premium research-team introduction:
- central invitation:
  - `Meet your analyst team`
- a secondary line beneath:
  - `Elite multi-agent research desk`
- four larger specialist cards around the center
- each card includes:
  - one icon
  - one role title
  - one short function phrase
- motion should feel like a composed lineup reveal, not a UI pop-in

## Agent cards
- `Fundamental / Macro`
  - icon idea: globe / regime ring
- `News Impact`
  - icon idea: signal pulse / event burst
- `Sentiment`
  - icon idea: crowd wave / speech arc
- `Technical`
  - icon idea: candlestick / chart glyph

## Motion direction
- Phase 2 contracts upward and fades into the background instead of hard-cutting.
- The team scene expands to a wider, more dominant footprint.
- Center title resolves first.
- The four cards arrive in controlled sequence with subtle connector lines.
- Final hold should feel like a polished analyst roster.

## Constraints
- Low information density
- Large readable labels
- Strong finance identity
- No dashboard in phase 3
- GIF-safe gradients and motion only
- Slightly shorter total runtime than v5
