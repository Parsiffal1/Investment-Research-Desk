This desk is built to help a researcher read markets, not to replace them. Price structure, macro pressure, sentiment, and disagreement are all kept visible instead of being flattened into a single confident answer.

The result is a more inspectable financial workflow: one that can look at cross-asset motion, keep track of uncertainty, and leave behind artifacts worth reviewing after the run.
